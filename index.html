04 / FAQs

COMMON QUESTIONS

How do I book an appointment?

You can book directly through WhatsApp by clicking the 'Book Now' button, or call us during studio hours. We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance.

How much does a tattoo cost?

Pricing varies based on size, detail, and placement. We offer free consultations to discuss your design and provide an accurate quote.

How should I prepare for my session?

Get a good night's sleep, eat a full meal beforehand, stay hydrated, and avoid alcohol for 24 hours prior. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the tattoo area.

How long does a session take?

Small pieces may take 1–2 hours, while larger or more detailed work can take multiple sessions of 3–5 hours each.

What is your aftercare process?

We provide detailed aftercare instructions after every session. Generally, keep the area clean, moisturized, and out of direct sunlight for at least 2 weeks.

Do you do cover-ups?

Yes, we specialize in cover-up work. Book a consultation so we can assess the existing tattoo and discuss design options.
